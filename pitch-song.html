Updated Pitch Flow

This form now collects only what your team actually needs: the song, a strong written pitch, and one Google Drive link containing all required photos, videos, and Spotify Canvas assets. That keeps the website light while still giving your team everything needed to download and pitch fast.

Step 01
Song Selection

Pick the exact unreleased track you want us to pitch.

Recommended pitching window: 7 to 28 days before release.

Step 02
Your Pitch
Write a curator-friendly story

Tell the story behind the song in a clear, human way. Mention the emotion, artist story, theme, sound, and where the track fits. Avoid one-line promo copy. Write it like a strong editorial pitch.

Highlighted Example Pitch

"Midnight Echoes" is a late-night Hindi pop record built around heartbreak, reflection, and quiet confidence. The production stays cinematic and modern, with airy synths, intimate vocals, and a hook designed to stay with the listener after the first play. The song works especially well for emotional pop, late-night drive, heartbreak, and mood playlists. If you like tracks that balance vulnerability with polish, this release lands in that zone.

0 characters
Include the story Why was the song made, and what should a curator feel while listening?
Describe the sound Call out genre, vibe, instrumentation, or energy in plain language.
Name the fit Say what listeners or playlist moments this record is best for.
Step 03
Artist Media Requirements

Instead of uploading heavy files on the website, ask the artist to place everything in one shareable Google Drive folder. Your team can directly download from there, which keeps this page lightweight and avoids server load.

4 HD / 4K Photos

Clear artist photos for pitching and platform support.

  • Total 4 photos required.
  • Front-facing shots only.
  • At least 90% face visibility.
  • No side-view or heavily covered face shots.

4 to 5 Video Samples

Artist should introduce themselves, their song, and platform mentions on camera.

  • Total 4 to 5 short videos.
  • Artist should mention their own name.
  • Artist should mention the song name.
  • Artist should mention target platforms where needed.

Spotify Canvas

One vertical looping visual for Spotify.

  • Duration: 7 to 9 seconds max.
  • Format: 9:16 vertical.
  • Should be clean, branded, and ready to publish.

Make sure the folder permission is set so the Tunefry team can open and download files.
